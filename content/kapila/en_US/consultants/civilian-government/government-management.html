Government Management

Improving government management of key services

Federal agencies that deliver benefits and entitlements to citizens, administer grants, and provide core services to other agencies have similar government management responsibilities. Not only must they operate efficiently to better serve citizen and agency customers, they must also set policy and implement key federal initiatives.

Booz Allen delivers innovative technology and management solutions such as shared services and cloud computing that help our clients streamline services and improve government management.

Our worked has helped organizations such as the General Services Administration (GSA) set the direction for high-performance green buildings. We also provided technology infrastructure for the Presidential Transition for GSA; and we helped the Department of Labor develop and advance GovBenefits.gov to provide citizens with better access to federal benefits information.
