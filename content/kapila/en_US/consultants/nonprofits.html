Nonprofit Organizations

Management and technology solutions to support the special missions of nonprofit organizations

Nonprofit organizations face many of the same complex issues that confront their commercial counterparts, though typically with fewer resources to address them.

From fund raising to managing a lean (and often volunteer) staff to collaborating with partners from the public, private, and civil sectors, many nonprofit organizations require management, leadership, and technical solutions tailored to their specific mission and environment.

Booz Allen offers strategic vision, management prowess, and change management expertise to help nonprofit organizations achieve their mission goals. As leaders in developing the megacommunity approach, we have been especially effective in helping nonprofit organizations work closely with partners in business, government, and the community to address social problems beyond the scope of their programs but that directly advance their mission.

We also provide strategic support and technical expertise to help organizations leverage information technologies to manage and scale constituent services, expand subscriptions, engage members, and improve fund raising. Our strong cyber expertise helps nonprofit organizations mitigate increasingly sophisticated cybersecurity risks while facilitating integration and unity in today’s interconnected environment.

Corporate social responsibility has been and continues to be embedded in the culture of Booz Allen’s people. Our professionals bring a special passion to our work with nonprofit organizations, recognizing that we are helping make a positive and long-term impact– for ourselves and for our communities.

Associations, foundations, charities, museums, art institutes, and research and academic institutions all draw on Booz Allen’s understanding of the nonprofit world and deeply rooted experience in business and operational strategy to help them address their toughest problems. With nearly a century of management consulting experience, we bring best practices from the nonprofit, business, and government organizations we serve.
