Civil Agencies

Intelligence to Operations – Continuous Diagnostics, Monitoring, Mitigation, and Measurement

The interconnectivity of global networks has changed the nature of cyber threats. In addition to hactivists intent on mischief, organized criminal groups and nation-state adversaries seek intelligence, financial gain, and government and corporate secrets. Staying ahead of these increasingly sophisticated cyber adversaries requires four things:

  1. Timely, accurate threat intelligence and it’s potential mission impact
  2. The ability to make mission decisions, using observed data
  3. Tradecraft to execute organization-wide changes in near real time
  4. An operations approach to monitor and inform cyber efforts

Booz Allen’s “intelligence to operations” lifecycle is a pro-active approach to protect organizations. Key to this is enabling data-driven decisions based on threat intelligence and telemetry from IT infrastructure. Fusing these two types of data together allows government agencies to anticipate and prioritize threats, and take pre-attack defensive actions to maintain an effective security posture.

A Continuous Diagnostics and Mitigation (CDM) solution is a critical element in our intelligence to operations lifecycle. CDM allows government agencies to:

  • More actively combat threats by shifting cybersecurity best practices from a compliance reporting model to a real-time monitoring approach
  • Provide access to services that help agencies strategically plan and implement an Information Security and Continuous Monitoring (ISCM) program with the least amount of disruption and cost
  • Access discounted CDM tools and services, allowing more value for your investment

International Cybersecurity

Enabling The Next Wave of Innovation

In a world where everything and everybody is connected, our future is tied to the access, availability and synthesis of information. That is why cybersecurity cannot be treated as an afterthought. And it cannot be achieved through technology alone.

With a multidisciplinary approach, Booz Allen provides robust cybersecurity solutions to a broad range of clients and industries, enabling them to confidently pursue the opportunities offered by the cyber revolution.

Cybersecurity is a mission enabler. Without it, sophisticated information-sharing networks—which are already addressing some of our most pressing challenges—will not succeed. And yet protecting these networks requires an organization-wide effort.

It all starts with a mission-driven cyber policy, which in turn guides cyber operations. At the same time, operations are enabled by investments in people and technology. The entire process is supported by cyber management, and unified—into a holistic cybersecurity platform—through cybersecurity mission integration.
