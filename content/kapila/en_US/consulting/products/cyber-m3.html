Creating a Tailored “Opportunities Roadmap” to Cybersecurity

It is critical that organizations fully understand and manage the growing risk posed by cyber threats today. To create a clear, comprehensive picture, Booz Allen assesses and benchmarks an organization’s cybersecurity programs, then develops an enterprise-wide portfolio of capabilities tailored to resources and priorities.

One of the first steps is to determine precisely where an organization’s cybersecurity strengths and weaknesses lie. We work with the internal cybersecurity team to evaluate how the organization’s cyber technology is used, where it is vulnerable, and whether undetected malware has already breached its defenses.

In the next phase, Booz Allen helps organizations develop a detailed blueprint and timeline for transforming their cybersecurity environment from the ground up. This process leverages the latest industry best practices, as well as the expertise and lessons learned – across all sectors of business and government – of Booz Allen’s more than 5,000 experienced cyber professionals.

Booz Allen’s unique approach helps clients measure their current security plans, manage immediate and potential threats and build a mature a framework for an optimal, lasting cybersecurity solution.

The Booz Allen approach:

  • Identifies gaps and risks in current cyber security programs.
  • Creates a strategic path for managing real risks from advanced threats.
  • Assesses performance across all of the aspects of a cybersecurity framework--the people, process, and technology.
  • Moves client capabilities from current state to a risk-aligned target state.
  • Identifies a tailored “opportunities roadmap” that specifies solutions for an integrated cyber security program.
