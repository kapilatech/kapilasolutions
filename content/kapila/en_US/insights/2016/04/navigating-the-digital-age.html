Navigating the Digital Age

The Definitive Cybersecurity Guide for Directors and Officers

Booz Allen is a contributor to recent publication, Navigating the Digital Age: The Definitive Cybersecurity Guide for Directors and Officers. Led by Palo Alto Networks, this book provides boards, executives and officers at enterprises, government agencies and other organizations with practical, expert advice on how to best protect their businesses from cyberattacks.

Explore the chapters contributed by Booz Allen below:
  • Securing Privacy And Profit In The Era Of Hyperconnectivity And Big Data
  • Developing A Cybersecurity Strategy: Thrive In An Evolving Threat Environment
  • Designing A Cyber Fusion Center: A Unified Approach With Diverse Capabilities
  • Supply Chain As An Attack Chain
  • Planning, Preparation, And Testing For An Enterprise-Wide Incident Response
  • Cybersecurity Readiness Through Workforce Development

Navigating the Digital Age: The Definitive Cybersecurity Guide for Directors and Officers is available for complete download at www.securityroundtable.org.
