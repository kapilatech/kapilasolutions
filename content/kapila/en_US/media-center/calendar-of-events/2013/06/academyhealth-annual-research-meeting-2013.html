AcademyHealth Annual Research Meeting

June 22-24, 2013

Baltimore Convention Center | Baltimore, MD

Booz Allen Executive Advisor Joachim Roski will moderate a panel at the AcademyHealth Annual Research Meeting titled “Big Data for Health Services Research.”

Current IT infrastructures face three major challenges: 1) Volume - lack of storage capacity and analytic capability to handle massive volumes of data; 2) Variety - data comes in many different formats, which can be difficult and expensive to integrate and 3) Velocity - the inability to process and analyze data in real time prevents the extraction of the most value from available data.

Panelists include Niall Brennan, Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services; Basit Chaudhry, University of California, Los Angeles; Graham Gardner, Kyruus; and Darren Schulte, Resolution Health, Inc. They will highlight developments in these areas and discuss how health care and health services research are taking advantage of next-generation data management and analysis.
