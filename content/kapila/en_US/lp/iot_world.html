Internet of Things World Conference

Welcome to the Connected Ecosystem

Booz Allen Hamilton is a proud sponsor of Internet of Things World 2015 in San Francisco, California, May 12-13, 2015. IoT World is the largest and most comprehensive global IoT event with more than 4,000 attendees, including 250+ industry thought leaders and 150+ exhibitors. As an integrated solutions provider, Booz Allen looks forward to engaging with participants from across the IoT ecosystem to imagine and advance strategies to further the IoT revolution while protecting from the expanding cyber attack surface.
