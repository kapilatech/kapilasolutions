Would you like to receive additional information from Booz Allen?

You are cordially invited to join Booz Allen Hamilton at the Undersea Technology Innovation Day on Friday, August 21st.

Both afloat and ashore, organizations like yours are being asked to do more with less. Today's environment requires you to manage vast amounts of data, coordinate across various tools and technologies, and make informed decisions to support our sailors in their missions with fewer available resources. These innovative products and managed services help address the challenges you face because they're developed using a deep understanding of your needs and mission requirements.

Come experience an exclusive preview of an array of specialized products and services from Booz Allen and our trusted partners Asset, Mikel and Sea Corp. Explore our culture of innovation and grab some lunch at Half Street “Truckeroo” across from the office. We look forward to seeing you.

Friday, August 21st, 2015

Booz Allen Hamilton 20 M Street, SE Office; Washington, DC
9:00AM - 3:00PM

Event Preview

Demonstrations

Enjoy an exclusive opportunity to browse through and experience hands-on demos of new products, and get a sneak peek of products still in development. Our products address the challenges you face because they’re developed from a deep understanding of your needs and mission requirements.

Detect and Collect to track down the culprit. For clients who need to view what was previously undetectable, our Detect and Collect products allow you to track clues and deduce the culprit.

Anticipate and Prevent to stop threats before they happen. For clients who need to anticipate organizational threats before they happen, our Anticipate and Prevent products defend clients against a host of potential threat actors.

Activate and Enhance to inform data-driven decisions. For clients who need to make data-driven decisions, our Activate and Enhance products harness the power of analytics.

Connect and Visualize to create strong networks of vital information. For clients who need to access information immediately, our Connect and Visualize products open up channels of communication and provide context to what was previously abstract.

Building Directions

Upon entering the 20 M Street Building, proceed to the front desk and show your identification. They will have a list of all registered attendees. Company Demonstration About
ASSETT Advanced Command and Control System
 Uses a “Double Helix” data fusion process to identify key information to enhance mission effectiveness.
Integrated Command and Display (ICAD) ICAD is a system that readily adapts to multiple platform types with diverse sensors.
Sea Corp Anti-Torpedo Torpedo (ATT) Launcher This technology is based on the employment of automotive airbag inflators as the impulse energy to launch the ATT.
MDA Valve Developing potential replacement hardware technologies for pyro-valves, bolt cutters, and cable cutters, all using automotive airbag inflators as the energetics behind these mechanical devices.
Mikel Advanced Contact Management System Revolutionary approach to developing the tactical picture; the key information used for command decision making, post mission reporting, safe maneuvering of the platform and effective employment of weapon payloads.
Submerged Acoustic Navigation System (SANS) Helps to determine a vehicle position in real time to improve navigation accuracy.
Booz Allen Hamilton Project Marlin Provides a 3-D underwater navigation perspective.
Precision Navigation
(P-NAV)		 A product that uses an RF receiver in the GPS frequency bands to receive signals
while submerged.
Raptor An innovative simulation tool for analyzing your system's reliability, operational costs, and capacity.
Polaris™ A real-time project analysis solution that integrates cost, schedule and risk into a single, analytical model allowing users to stop project cost and schedule growth before they occur.
Tableau By incorporating data from multiple sources, Tableau reports allow us and the customer to focus in on bottlenecks and data gaps.
Underwater Acoustics Analytics Open source implementation of underwater acoustic signature detection and identification.
